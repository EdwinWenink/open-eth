OpenEth

OpenEth enables the analysis of ethical dilemmas of a particular type, or types, and the discovery of a decision principle to resolve them that is consistent with information gleaned from them.

We contend that all behavior of autonomous systems should be guided by explicit ethical principles determined through a consensus of ethicists.

Such principles ensure the ethical behaviour of complex and dynamic systems and further serve as a basis for justification of their actions as well as a control abstraction for managing unanticipated behaviour. To provide assistance in developing ethical principles, in particular those pertinent to the behaviour of autonomous systems, we have developed OpenEth, a general ethical dilemma analyser that, through a dialogue with ethicists, codifies ethical principles in any given domain.
